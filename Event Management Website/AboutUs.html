How It Works

When you book with Elevated Events, you can feel confident that you will be working with experienced, professional wedding planners and coordinators who are just as excited about your wedding day as you are. If you are looking for full planning and design work, you will be working with Jenn Rosen, Owner & Principal Planner. If event management would be a better fit, you will work with one of our wonderful team members. Upon booking with us, we will put you in contact with your wedding coordinator who will be with you every step of the way. 


Elevated Events is known for warm customer service and going above and beyond to make your day magical. We have been so blessed to gather an amazing team of passionate and dedicated wedding planners and coordinators with years of experience to ensure your planning experience is enjoyable and stress free.
